Collections, whose elements can be filtered.

separate and filter and the others are expressed in terms of each other. As you would expect. I am lazy here to unfold this relationship formally.

Laws:

  • The [intuitive] relationship between separate and filter and the others.
  • map Just >>> filter = map
  • map join >>> filter = filter >>> filter
