Loadable profunctors

class Profunctor p => LoadableP p #

Can load the profunctor to carry additional info in product structure. Known as "strong" in the Haskell community.

  • "p" means "product"
  • "e" means "early" position of the carried load in the product structure.
  • "l" means "late" position of the carried load in the product structure.

class Profunctor p => LoadableS p #

Can load the profunctor to carry additional info in sum structure. Known as "choice" in the Haskell community.

  • "s" means "sum"
  • "e" means "early" position of the carried load in the sum structure.
  • "l" means "late" position of the carried load in the sum structure.
